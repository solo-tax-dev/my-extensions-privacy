Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

This privacy policy covers the following browser extensions: StashPile, SnapPolish, and ShopScope.

Summary

None of these extensions collect, transmit, sell, or share any personal data, browsing history, or usage information. All data these extensions create or read stays stored locally on your own device, inside your browser's local storage.

StashPile

StashPile reads the titles and URLs of tabs you choose to "stash" so it can save and restore them later. This data is stored only in your browser's local storage and is never sent anywhere. Nothing is transmitted off your device. Exporting a backup file saves it directly to your own computer; it does not pass through any server we control.

SnapPolish

SnapPolish captures an image of the current tab only when you click its icon, so it can let you style and download that image. The captured image is processed entirely on your device and is never uploaded or transmitted anywhere.

ShopScope

ShopScope reads publicly visible listing information (titles, prices, review counts) from Etsy search result pages you are already viewing, in order to display market statistics. This analysis happens entirely on your device. No data is transmitted to us or any third party, and the extension requests no special browser permissions.
